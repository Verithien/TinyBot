Blizzard Bot

What does it do?

Blizzard Bot is a Discord bot used to attain information regarding Blizzard games for the user with a much quicker response than having to search the web.

Commands

Commands

Character

<command> <realm> <character> <region>

Guild

<command> <realm> - <guild> <region>

Realm

<command> <realm> <region>

Hero

<command> <account> <region> <platform> <mode>

MMR

<command> <account> <region>

COMMAND

DESCRIPTION

EXAMPLE
?char Gets brief summary of character ?char thrall chueey us
?ilvl Shows equipped/average ilvl ?ilvl thrall chueey us
?gear Shows gear in each slot ?gear thrall chueey us
?tals Shows main spec talents ?tals thrall chueey us
?stats Shows character stats ?stats thrall chueey us
?prog Shows character raid progression ?prog thrall chueey us
?pvp Shows PvP Ratings ?pvp thrall chueey us
?moose Shows if character has Arch moose ?moose thrall chueey us
?glory Shows if character as Glory: HFC ?glory thrall chueey us
?guildrank Shows guilds HFC progression ?guildrank thrall - reforged us
?status Shows Online or Offline ?status reforged us
?heroname Shows stats for specific hero ?hanzo BlizzBot#0000 us pc qp(cp for competitive)
?hots Shows Heroes of the Storm MMR ?hots BlizzBot#0000 us
